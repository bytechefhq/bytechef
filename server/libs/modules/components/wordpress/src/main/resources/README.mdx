### Permalink Setup

1. Navigate to your dashboard.
2. Click on **Settings**.
3. Click on **Permalinks**.
4. Select **Post name**.
5. Click **Save Changes**.

## Connection Setup

### Find Application Passwords

1. Navigate to your dashboard.
2. Download the plugin from: https://github.com/WP-API/Basic-Auth (Click on Code -> Download Zip).
3. Click on **Plugins**.
4. Click on **Add Plugin**.
5. Click on **Upload Plugin". And upload previously downloaded plugin in .zip format.
6. After uploading click on **Install Now**.
7. Click on **Activate Plugin**.
8. Click on **Users**.
9. Click on **Profile**.
10. Here you can see Application Passwords.
