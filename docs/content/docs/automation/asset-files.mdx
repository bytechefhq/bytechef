title: "Asset Files"
description: Upload and manage files that workflows and the AI Hub can read and produce.
---

> **Coming soon.** Asset Files are on the upcoming release track and are not yet available in the latest released version of ByteChef.

**Asset Files** is the workspace's file library — a place to upload files once and reuse them across automations, and to find the files your runs produce.

- **Upload** files by dragging them onto the page or browsing; uploaded files are tagged as user uploads.
- **Browse and preview** files with their source, size, and timestamps; open a file's detail view to inspect it.
- **Reuse** files in workflows and AI Hub conversations instead of re-uploading per run.
