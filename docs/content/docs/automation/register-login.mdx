## Register an Account

1. Go to [https://www.bytechef.io/](https://www.bytechef.io/).
2. Click **Sign up**.
3. Enter your email address and click **Continue**.
4. Create a password that meets the password requirements, then click **Continue with password**.
5. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to verify your account.
   - If you donâ€™t see the email, check your spam or junk folder.
6. After verification, click **Start** to finish setting up your account.
7. You can now sign in using your new credentials.

## Log In

1. Go to [https://www.bytechef.io/](https://www.bytechef.io/).
2. Click **Login**.
3. Enter your email address and password.
4. Click **Log in** to access your workspace.
   - Forgot your password? Use the **Forgot password** link on the sign-in page to reset it.
