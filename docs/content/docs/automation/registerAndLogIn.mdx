## Register Account

1. Navigate to [link](https://www.bytechef.io/).
2. Click on "Sign Up".
3. Enter your email.
4. Click on "Continue".
5. Enter password that conform to the guidelines.
6. Click on "Continue with password".
7. Confirmation email is sent to your email address.
8. This is verification email click on URL to complete your registration.
9. Account registration is compleated, click on "Start".
10. Login in ByteChef using your newly created credentials.

## Log In

1. Navigate to [link](https://www.bytechef.io/).
2. Click on "Login".
3. Enter your email address and password.
4. Click on "Log in".
